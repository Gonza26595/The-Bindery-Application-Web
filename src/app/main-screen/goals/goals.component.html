Goals

Bindery Knowledge Group defines itself as a “ crowd-sourced and crowd-funded consulting and advocacy group that brings the world's socially minded talent together to solve our time's most challenging education obstacles. We are a community of thinkers moving communities forward”. The main objectives of this company are:

• Learning Solutions for the Educational Institution
• Learning Solutions for the Non-Profit Organization
• Learning Solutions for the Start-Up Company

The Bindery, the official journal, shares the same goals but at the same time has the important task to deliver information about the progress that different entrepreneurships have all around the world –thanks to BKG’s assessment- and provide articles related to the human narrative.

In order to wake empathy and commitment, writers, journalists and entrepreneurs from different parts of the globe are invited to write about their realities, their ideas and their victories. We encourage people to write and share their knowledge for others to understand each community, each culture, every part of the world.

So, we invite you to share your outlook and your lessons. The Bindery has the mission of transforming the world into a space where people can grow to be the best version of themselves as well as the best companions to others.
